Our Process

Depending on your needs, we will implement the part of the process that suits your project the most. Namely, we understand that every project is different. We need to get to know your project in depth as if it was our own idea.

  • Discovery

    Discovery phase is when we poke and test your idea, explore it and learn it, using tools like: user personas, user flows, sitemap/app map, wireframes. Once this is done with, both yourself and we will have the clarity needed to start the production process.

    • Problem to be solved
    • User personas
    • Goals and tasks
    • User flows
    • Sitemap
    • Low fidelity wireframes

  • Production

    Production phase is where we make the final designs and the development of your product, test it and deploy it. This is when you see your idea come to life.

    • High fidelity wireframes
    • UI design
    • Frontend development
    • Backend development
    • Quality assurance

  • Maintenance

    Post-production means we will still be there for you after the active development already ended, to help you with the maintenance if needed and provide support if something goes wrong unexpectedly.

    • Bugfixing
    • Updates
    • Maintenance

  • SEO services

    SEO services help to ensure that a site is accessible to a search engine and improves the chances that the site will be found and ranked highly by the search engine.

    • Site audits
    • Competitor & Marketplace analysis
    • Structural & Tech optimisation
    • SEO content
    • Digital PR & Outreach
    • Local SEO
    • SEO Consultancy
